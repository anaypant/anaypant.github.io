NOTES FOR SPACE INVADERS

Random Aliens Spawn at Random Columns
Player can move left/right/up/down and fire
Player can Pick up Powerups - maybe later?
Player has to destroy spaceships
Everytime a spaceship is destroyed, counter goes up --> increased difficulty every increment of destroyed ships
player loses if they touch ship or ship reaches bottom

Inps and outputs
x dist to closest enemy
y dist to closest enemy
x dist to second closest enemy : -1 if non applicable
y dist to second closest enemy : -1 if non applicable
outputs: w, a, s, d, fire
